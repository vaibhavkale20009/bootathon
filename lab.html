TITLE:DETERMINATION OF RADIUS OF WIRE BY USING REFRACTION

AIM:

the aim of this practical is to study refraction
and to determine the radius to thin wire from the light's angle of refraction
when it hits the wire.

THEORY:

Light gets bend towards the corners of steep objects
This phenomenon is used to determine the radius of thin wire.

PROCEDURE:

1.setup the optical bench with fine adjustments of the slits and microscope
2.move the wire gradually by 15cms intervals
3.At each station take a reading of the angle of refraction
4.By taking the average at each point of reading the approx diameter can be obtained
